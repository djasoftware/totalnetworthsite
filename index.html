Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

We do not collect any data. All your information stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

  • No personal data collected
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party services
  • No ads or cookies

Storage

All data (assets, liabilities, goals, settings, PIN) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. We cannot access it.

PIN Security

Your PIN is never stored as plain text. It uses SHA256 hashing with a unique salt.

Deleting Data

Uninstall the app to delete all data. We keep no backups.

Children

We do not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
